## What to Look For

When deciding who to follow, look for people who:

| Good Sign | Red Flag |
|-----------|----------|
| Posts about actual projects | Only shares articles |
| Shares code snippets | Sells courses aggressively |
| Engages with comments | Never replies |
| Has 1k-50k followers | 500k+ followers (too busy to reply) |
| Posts 2-3x/week | Posts 10x/day |
| Shows metrics/results | Makes vague claims |
