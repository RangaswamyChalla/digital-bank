# LinkedIn Outreach Templates — Rangaswamy Challa

## Quick Reference

- **Tone:** Friendly, professional, helpful — not salesy
- **Length:** 50-150 words max
- **Goal:** Start conversation, not close a sale
- **Follow-up:** Send 1 reminder after 4-5 days, then stop

## Key Rules

1. **Personalize** — Always reference something specific about them
2. **Be helpful** — Offer value before asking
3. **Keep it short** — Decision makers have 30 seconds
4. **No attachments in first message** — Builds mistrust
5. **Follow up once** — Then stop (3 messages max total)
6. **Match their energy** — Casual tone if they're casual
